# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The BMAD team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. Thank you for improving the security of our project. We appreciate your efforts and responsible disclosure and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please email our security team at `security@bmad.ai` and include the word "SECURITY" in the subject line.

We will endeavor to respond within 24 hours.

## PGP Key for Security Communications

To encrypt your communications with the security team, please use our official PGP key.

**Note:** The following PGP key is an example and should **not** be used for reporting vulnerabilities.

Example PGP Key: `0xBMAD1234`

The official PGP key can be found on our website at `https://bmad.ai/security` or from public keyservers.
